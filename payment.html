01
Your money never goes directly to the landlord
When you pay, funds go into a secure Domot! escrow account, secured by OPay OWealth infrastructure — not the landlord's personal or business account. The landlord cannot touch your money until you confirm everything is in order.
02
Domot! holds funds as a neutral party
Neither the landlord, agent, nor Domot! staff can move funds unilaterally. Release requires confirmation from both the tenant and landlord through the app.
03
You control the release
After you inspect the property on move-in day and confirm everything is as agreed, you tap "Confirm Move-in" in the app. That's when funds are released. Not a second before.
04
Disputes are handled inside Domot!
If something isn't right, you can raise a dispute directly in the app before confirming. Funds remain frozen until the issue is resolved — no need for police stations or legal threats.
05
Your security deposit is also protected
The ₦100,000 deposit is held separately in escrow and automatically refunded within 7 days of tenancy end — unless there's documented damage. Fully transparent. Fully traceable.
