1. Parties

This Tenancy Agreement is made between John Etim ("Landlord") and Prince Frank ("Tenant") for the property located at Shelter Afrique Estate, Uyo, Akwa Ibom State.

2. Property

The Landlord agrees to let and the Tenant agrees to take the self-contained apartment described as Unit 4B, Ground Floor, Block C, Shelter Afrique Estate, Uyo.

3. Term

The tenancy shall commence on August 15, 2026 and shall continue for a period of one (1) year, unless terminated in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement.

4. Rent

The annual rent is ₦450,000 (Four Hundred and Fifty Thousand Naira), payable in advance before or on the commencement date.

5. Security Deposit

A security deposit of ₦100,000 shall be paid by the Tenant. This deposit shall be refundable at the end of the tenancy, subject to deductions for damages beyond fair wear and tear.

6. Renewal

Either party may terminate this agreement by giving thirty (30) days written notice. Renewal shall be subject to mutual agreement and may involve rent review.

7. Use of Premises

The premises shall be used solely for residential purposes. Subletting without prior written consent is prohibited.

8. Maintenance

The Landlord shall be responsible for structural repairs. The Tenant shall maintain the interior and report any defects promptly.

9. Dispute Resolution

Any dispute arising from this agreement shall first be resolved through mediation. Failing that, the parties submit to the jurisdiction of the courts in Akwa Ibom State.

10. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.
