Lease Terms

This tenancy agreement commences on 15 August 2026 and terminates on 14 August 2027, constituting a fixed-term lease of one (1) year. The lease may be renewed by mutual written agreement no fewer than 30 days prior to expiration.

  • Fixed-term: 12 months
  • Auto-renewal requires written confirmation
  • Holdover tenancy charged at 125% of monthly rate
Property Rules

The tenant shall keep the property in a clean and habitable condition at all times, and must not make structural alterations without the landlord's prior written consent.

  • No unauthorized subletting
  • Pets by written agreement only
  • Noise curfew: 10 PM – 7 AM
  • Outdoor spaces kept tidy at all times
Maintenance Responsibilities

The landlord is responsible for structural maintenance and major repairs. The tenant is responsible for minor day-to-day maintenance and keeping fixtures in working order.

  • Landlord: plumbing, roofing, structural integrity
  • Tenant: light fixtures, door handles, minor leaks
  • Tenant reports damage within 48 hours of discovery
Payment Terms

Annual rent of ₦650,000 is payable in advance. A security deposit of ₦65,000 is due upon signing. Late payment attracts a 5% penalty after a 7-day grace period.

  • Rent due: 15th of each applicable month (if split)
  • Accepted payment via Domot! platform
  • Receipts issued within 24 hours
Termination Conditions

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days' written notice. Early termination by the tenant forfeits the security deposit. The landlord may terminate immediately for material breach.

  • 30 days notice required from either party
  • Property returned in original condition
  • Deposit refunded within 14 days of key handover
