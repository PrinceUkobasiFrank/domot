Property Details
4-bedroom duplex located at Shelter Afrique Estate, Uyo, Akwa Ibom State. Property includes all fixtures, fittings, and appurtenances. Total land area: 450 sqm. Title: Certificate of Occupancy verified.
Purchase Terms
Buyer agrees to purchase the property for ₦65,000,000. A deposit of 10% (₦6,500,000) is required within 48 hours of agreement execution. Balance of ₦58,500,000 due at closing.
Transfer Conditions
Transfer of ownership shall occur only after: (a) full payment verification, (b) Ministry of Lands documentation review, (c) Domot! escrow release confirmation. Seller warrants clear title free of encumbrances.
Buyer Responsibilities
Buyer shall: (a) provide valid identification, (b) fund deposit within 48 hours, (c) complete balance payment within 30 days, (d) cooperate with documentation transfer, (e) pay all applicable taxes and fees.
Seller Responsibilities
Seller shall: (a) provide all original property documents, (b) execute deed of assignment, (c) obtain consent to assign from relevant authorities, (d) deliver vacant possession at closing, (e) warrant no undisclosed encumbrances.
Dispute Resolution
Any dispute shall first be mediated through Domot! dispute resolution. Failing resolution, parties submit to arbitration under the Arbitration and Mediation Act 2023. Venue: Uyo, Akwa Ibom State.
